Welcome to Qdenga Booth!

It is an honour to be participating in the Indonesia Pediatric Society’s (IDAI) biggest event this year.

Dengue is one of the world's major public health threats and Indonesia is among the most affected countries.

Everyone is at risk of dengue regardless of where they live, age, or lifestyle.

We can fight dengue together and achieve Indonesia’s goal of zero dengue death by 2030!

We can do so by driving awareness, intensifying vector control, and leveraging innovative preventive measures including vaccination as the key to strengthening current dengue prevention the Indonesian people to protect themselves from dengue.

Takeda is committed to bringing a well-tolerated, proven-efficacy vaccine, Qdenga, to help patients who will benefit against dengue.

Qdenga is the first dengue vaccine without screening requirements and can be given to people aged 6-45 years old.

Pediatricians own a crucial role in educating the importance of dengue prevention. Moving forward we hope to continue collaborating with IDAI to protect more children from dengue.

Qdenga is coming really soon to the country, therefore we cordially invite you to join our launch event in Indonesia on the 5th of February 2023, you can find further information in this booth.
